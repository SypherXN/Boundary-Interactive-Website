Ahead of USC Games Expo, we completed a focused stabilization pass after a long period of package inactivity, including dependency upgrades and compatibility checks to keep the build presentation-ready.

To make the expo demo easier for fast player rotation, we adapted the game flow to a single **Time Attack** mode, locked gameplay options for consistency, and introduced movable UI so players could position menus comfortably in their play space.

In parallel, we collaborated with **Yan YiAn** on refreshed logo and cover-art assets used in showcase materials.
